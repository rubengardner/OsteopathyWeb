Individual therapies

Osthepathy

We work with the principles of osteopathy. We believe the human body has all it needs to cure itself, unblocking it to regain fluidity. Techniques vary from low amplitude-high-velocity thrust, which we would use in the spine, to more subtle techniques with force as low as 5 grams for structures such as the fascia or the cranium. It’s a holistic approach, where the body is tested to know what is necessary to bring it into alignment

Other therapies

We use other therapies to complete our work. We mainly use deep tissue massage, dry needling and cupping. These techniques are of greater intensity and are used on individuals who need an extra push to regain health, especially the dry needling and cupping. We try to use our hands all the time, but sometimes the help of needle or a cup is necessary for the situation involved.

Nutrition

It’s the foundation of the self. Manual therapy and movement don’t have the same effect on the internal chemistry as what we ingest. Each nutrition plan is tailored to the individual, taking into account the patient's goals. However, well-being and longevity are always stressed in every plan. Especially importance is given to the well functioning of the organs and the nervous system, whilst maintaining a clean intercellular environment.
